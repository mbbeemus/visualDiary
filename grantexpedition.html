About the Grant Expedition Redesign

This project is my capstone project for the School of Media Arts and Design Interaction Design Concentration. In this project we were tasked with redesigning the Grant Expedition Website. Grant Expeditions is Tanzanian destination management company headquartered in Arusha. Their current site lacks standard UX design principles, has an inefficient booking management and inquiry handling system, and has an outdated design. Our task was to redesign the Grant Expeditions wesbite to improve both the user and the admin experiences.

Our Solution

Create a modern, secure, and user-friendly website that positions Grant Expedition as a trusted global tour provider.

  • Offer customizable itineraries that balance luxury, culture, and adventure.
  • Allow users to filter experiences by interest, destination, and comfort level.
  • Provide real-time availability and booking confirmation to reduce confusion.
  • Include personalized recommendations based on past searches or bookings.
  • Emphasize security, clarity, and storytelling through visuals, itineraries, and local insights.
